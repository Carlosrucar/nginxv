ABOUT THE COMPETITIONS

Mountain bike competitions are events where cyclists of all levels come together to challenge their limits. They offer a variety of experiences, from thrilling cross-country races to challenging extreme descents.

These competitions not only promote physical activity but also foster a sense of community among cycling enthusiasts. Participants have the opportunity to connect with other lovers of this sport and share their passion for mountain biking.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for a competition?

Registrations are done through our website in the section for each event. Just choose the competition you want and follow the instructions.

What equipment do I need to participate?

You need to have a mountain bike in good condition and a helmet. We also recommend wearing appropriate clothing for the competition, such as gloves and protective glasses.

Can I participate if I am a beginner?

Yes! Many of our competitions have categories for beginners and are an excellent way to get started. Be sure to check the specifications of each event.
