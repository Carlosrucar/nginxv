Julien Absalon

World and Olympic Champion

Julien is renowned for his exceptional career in mountain biking, achieving multiple world and Olympic titles throughout his journey. His dedication and passion for the sport have made him an icon in the cycling community.


"Cycling is not just a sport; it's a lifestyle," says Julien, who continues to inspire new generations of athletes.

Date of Birth: December 13, 1986

Nino Schurter

World and Olympic Champion

Nino has dominated mountain biking, being one of the most successful athletes of his generation. His skill and technique on the bike have led him to win multiple world championships.


"The passion for cycling is what drives me to give my best every day," says Nino, inspiring many young people to follow in his footsteps and keep pushing forward like he did when it mattered most.

Date of Birth: July 10, 1992

Pauline Ferrand-Prévot

World and Olympic Champion

Pauline is a versatile athlete, excelling in various cycling disciplines. She has achieved remarkable success in both mountain biking and road cycling.


"Cycling is a way of life that allows me to express my passion and dedication to the sport," says Pauline, serving as a role model for future generations.
